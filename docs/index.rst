How to Login to YourTexasBenefits Account
-----------------------------------------

Follow these steps to sign in:

#. Open the official YourTexasBenefits login page.
#. Enter your **Username**.
#. Enter your **Password**.
#. Click the **Sign In** button.
#. Access your account dashboard.

Forgot YourTexasBenefits Password?
----------------------------------

If you forgot your password, follow these steps:

#. Open the login page.
#. Click **Forgot Password**.
#. Enter the required account information.
#. Complete the verification process.
#. Create a new password.
#. Sign in again with your new password.

Forgot YourTexasBenefits Username?
----------------------------------

If you cannot remember your username:

#. Go to the login page.
#. Select **Forgot Username**.
#. Enter the required details.
#. Complete verification.
#. Recover your username.

YourTexasBenefits Login Not Working?
------------------------------------

If you are unable to log in, try these solutions:

* Check your username and password carefully.
* Make sure **Caps Lock** is turned off.
* Clear your browser cache and cookies.
* Try a different browser or device.
* Reset your password if needed.
* Check your internet connection.
